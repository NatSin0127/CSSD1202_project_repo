Purpose

To encourage sustainable living and instill eco-friendly habits in users to help better our environmental and ecological footprint. This will be done through the application by informing users on how to be environmentally conscious and offering various tools such as a tracking system to maintain and encourage their eco-friendly habits.

Target Audience

This application is aimed towards those who are curious of how to make better choices that do not harm the environment. This applies to people who want to be more knowledgeable and involved in being environmentally friendly as well as corporations who are aiming to improve their corporate social responsibility in terms of their environmental efforts.
