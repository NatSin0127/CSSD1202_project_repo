Tips for Sustainable Living

Below shows some simple tips on how you can live green, you can watch a video with further explaination of our tips by clicking on the icon next to it!


1. Use Less Plastic

Plastic takes years to break down and harms the environment. Carry a reusable bag, bottle, and utensils to reduce waste.

2. Save Energy at Home

Turn off lights when you leave a room. Use energy-efficient appliances and unplug devices when not in use.

3. Walk or Bike More

Cars release a lot of pollution. Walking, biking, or using public transport is better for the air and your health.

4. Buy Only What You Need

Overconsumption leads to waste. Think before you buy, choose quality over quantity, and support eco-friendly brands.

5. Eat More Plants

Meat production has a big carbon footprint. Eating more veggies and plant-based meals helps the planet.
