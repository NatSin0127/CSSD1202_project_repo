Quiz Purpose

This quiz is designed to help you realize what actions you make in your day-to-day life that help or harm the environment. We hope that you pick up some eco-friendly habits and drop harmful ones.

Quiz

1. Which of the following helps to reduce plastic usage?

a) Using plastic bags and bottles more.
b) Using reusable bags.
c) Using reusable water bottles.
d) Dumping plastic bags and bottles in the garbage.

2. What is the best form of transportation to reduce the carbon footprint?

a) Using buses.
b) Taking a taxi.
c) Riding bikes.
d) Taking the train.

3. Which of the following are the best methods to save water?

b) Leaving the faucet dripping.

d) Flushing the toilet unnecessarily.

d) Using lights that consume less electricity.
